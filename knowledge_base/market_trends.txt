King County Washington Real Estate Market Trends 2024

Seattle and surrounding King County areas have seen steady appreciation in property values over the past decade.
Average home prices in Seattle range from $600,000 to $900,000 depending on neighborhood.
Bellevue properties command premium prices often exceeding $1,000,000 due to proximity to tech companies.
Kent and Auburn offer more affordable options ranging from $400,000 to $600,000.
Redmond benefits from Microsoft headquarters proximity, driving demand and prices above market average.
The waterfront properties in any King County city command 20-40% premium over comparable non-waterfront homes.
Properties with view ratings of 3 or above sell for 10-25% more than zero-view equivalents.
King County median home price appreciation has averaged 6-8% annually over the last 5 years.
Low inventory continues to be a challenge with demand consistently exceeding supply in desirable zip codes.
Interest rate environment affects buyer purchasing power significantly. Higher rates reduce demand.
New construction has been limited, supporting existing home values.
Rental yields in King County range from 3% to 5% annually depending on property type and location.
